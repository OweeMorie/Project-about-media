Libertad National High School

Students at Libertad National High School, usually referred to as LINHS, are given
the opportunity to learn while still having fun at this educational institution.

About Us

Within the province of Misamis Oriental, you can find the LINHS in Poblacion, Libertad.
In addition to providing a challenging and nurturing atmosphere that encourages
academic performance, critical thinking, and learning that continues throughout one's
life, our objective is to instill a sense of leadership, creativity, and entrepreneurial spirit
in our students.

The Libertad National High School (LINHS) is a thriving community of learners that is
committed to educating students for a successful future in a world that is fast changing.
The school has a total enrollment of 353 students.

Academic Programs

We adapt to the specific interests and goals of each individual student at LINHS by
providing a wide variety of different streams to choose from.

1. STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.
2. ABM - Accountancy, Business, and Management.
3. HumSS - Humanities and Social Science.
4. EIM - Electrical Installation and Maintenance.
5. GAS - General Academic Strand.

Through its registration with the Department of Education (DepEd), LINHS
proves its dedication to provide an education that is of the highest possible
quality and satisfies the most stringent criteria.
